Outer Banks

Teen Drama / Adventure Creators: Jonas Pate, Josh Pate, & Shannon Burke

Set on a coastal town along the Outer Banks of North Carolina, the series follows a tight-knit group of working-class teenagers known as the "Pogues." They are determined to find out what happened to his missing father and locate a legendary treasure that he was searching for.

Their adventure puts them at odds with the wealthy, elite residents of the island, known as the "Kooks." As the Pogues follow a trail of long-buried secrets and clues, they navigate a dangerous web of greed, murder, and high-stakes rivalry. The quest for treasure is intertwined with first loves, broken friendships, and the challenges of coming of age in a town starkly divided by class.

"Outer Banks" is a perfect blend of mystery, adventure, and teen drama. With its beautiful coastal scenery and a compelling "us vs. them" storyline, the show is incredibly binge-worthy. It's an escapist adventure that captures the feeling of a permanent summer vacation filled with danger and excitement.
